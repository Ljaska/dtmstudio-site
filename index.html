Highlights

Multi‑segment Surfaces

Combine Asphere, Bevel, Flat — each with its own parameters and lengths.

Programmable Tool Radius

Explicit radius with clear M3/M4 modes. No legacy compensation overlap.

Per‑side Corrections

Apply MOD/XYZ per Left/Right/Average with % scaling and X‑shift.

SAPR

Smart Adaptive Profile Reconstruction — patent‑safe alternative to slope‑style methods.

Chamfer Automation

Automatic retract at chamfer end to protect tool edge.

Trajectory Visualization

Preview motion before cutting to boost first‑run confidence.
