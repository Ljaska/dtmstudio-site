About

DTM Studio is built by a precision optics CNC operator in a real SPDT production environment. The goal is clear: make everyday work faster, safer, and more intuitive while keeping full control over accuracy.

Focus areas include: multi‑segment generation, explicit tool radius, robust profile corrections (MOD/XYZ), and clean UI that helps operators avoid mistakes. The product evolves from real‑world feedback on the shop floor.

Location: Riga, Latvia • Status: Active development
