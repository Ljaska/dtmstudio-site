Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-23

This website uses only essential technologies required to deliver and protect the site. We do not use marketing trackers.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. Similar technologies may include local storage or security tokens.

2. Cookies and technologies we use

Essential only

  • Security and abuse prevention (e.g., DDoS protection)
  • Load balancing and performance
  • Basic functionality required to serve the website

Because this site uses Cloudflare for content delivery and security, Cloudflare may set essential cookies and process request data (such as IP address and request metadata) to protect and deliver the website.

3. No analytics / no ads

We do not intentionally use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics on this website.

4. How to control cookies

You can control or block cookies using your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact us using the email address published on this website.
